Over 1.5 billion people worldwide experience hearing loss, making everyday communication a significant challenge. Existing solutions such as basic speech-to-text tools only convert audio to text without providing emotional context, emergency awareness, or a mechanism for the hearing-impaired person to respond. There is no single, accessible, AI-powered platform that enables complete two-way communication while also interpreting the emotional tone of speech, detecting emergency situations, and displaying sign language in real time. VocalSync addresses this gap by combining machine learning-based emotion classification, emergency detection, real-time speech transcription, sign language visualization, and two-way voice reply — creating a complete, inclusive communication assistant that runs entirely on a standard laptop without any specialized hardware.
